# Grilled Alba

## TOOLS
- flamethrower
- Alba-holder

## INGREDIENTS
- Alba
- salt

## INSTRUCTIONS
- Put Alba on the Alba-holder
- Lightly cover Alba in salt
- Turn on the flamethrower
- Wait until Alba turns dark brown
- Take Alba off the Alba-holder
- Wait 10-15 minutes for Alba to cool down a little
- You are done
