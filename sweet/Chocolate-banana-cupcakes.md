# Chocolate Banana Cupcakes
## Description
What makes Banana-bread better? Right Chocolate! 

## Tools
fork
knive
mixer
bowl
cupcake shapes
oven

## ingredients:
- 250g Butter
- 300g Sugar
- 5 Eggs
- 4 Bananas
- 600g Flour
- 4Tsp baking powder 
- some vanilla(-extract/-sugar)
- 300g chocolate


## Instructions
1. peal bananas, mash with a fork and add to a big bowl
2. add flour, butter, sugar, vanilla, eggs and baking powder to the same bowl and mix
3. check the consitency
4. cut the chocolate into small pieces (1-3 cm^3) and add to the bowl
5. carefully mix again
6. fill the dough into the cupcake shapes
7. bake at 200C normal for 20min
8. let them cool down 
9. Enjoy!!! :D
