## Tools
food processor or blender
probably a pan

## Ingredients
FOR 10 PANCAKES nerds

2 BIG BANANAS ;)
2 eggs
2 dl oats
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
a bit of salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
butter
if you want, add cardamom or vanilla

## Instructions
1. put bananas, eggs, oats, cinnamon, salt, baking powder and cardamom in the food processor
2. blend for 2 minutes until you have a smooth batter
3. fry pancakes (1/2 dl per pancake) on a pan with butter until they are ready, about 3 minutes on each side
