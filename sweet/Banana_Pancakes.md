## Tools
food processor or blender
probably a pan

## Ingredients
FOR 10 PANCAKES nerds

2 BIG BANANAS ;)
2 eggs
2 dl oats
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
a bit of salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
butter
if you want, add cardamom or vanilla

## Instructions
1. put everything in the food processor
2. fry pancakes on a pan with butter until they are ready
