# Kanelbullar (sehr lecker)

## Description
This is my favorite recipe for cinnamon buns (and it is also the only one I tried). It can be made vegan very easily by just using vegan butter and some plant based milk (tbh I always use water and nobody notices...)

## Tools
- pot
- oven
- knive
- bowl
- get a job

## Ingredients
### For the dough
- 50g butter
- 170ml milk
- 50g sugar
- 20g yeast (fresh, however I always use dry yeast but you will have to do the conversion yourself)
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 teaspoons of cardamom (I never have cardamom whoops)
- 350g flour

### For the filling
- 30g butter
- 50g sugar
- 1 table spoon cinnamon
  
(tbh, this is never enough filling so do more - and especially more cinnamon)

## Instructions

1. For the dough, gently heat butter, milk, sugar, cardamom, and salt in a saucepan while stirring until the butter has completely melted. Let cool to body temperature.
3. Knead flour and the liquid together in a mixing bowl to form a dough. Cover and let rise for about 1 hour, until doubled in size.
6. Preheat the oven to 250°C (top and bottom heat). Brush the rolls with milk and bake for about 6 minutes until golden brown. Enjoy warm if possible.
4. Roll the dough out on a floured surface into a rectangle the size of a baking sheet. Melt the butter for the filling, spread it over the dough, then mix cinnamon and sugar and sprinkle evenly on top.
2. Crumble the yeast into small pieces and stir it into the warm liquid until dissolved. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
