# Kanelbullar (sehr lecker)

## Description
This is my favorite recipe for cinnamon buns (and it is also the only one I tried). It can be made vegan very easily by just using vegan butter and some plant based milk (tbh I always use water and nobody notices...)

## Tools
- pot
- oven
- knive
- bowl
- get a job

## Ingredients
### For the dough
- 50g butter
- 170ml milk
- 50g sugar
- 20g yeast (fresh, however I always use dry yeast but you will have to do the conversion yourself)
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 teaspoons of cardamom (I never have cardamom whoops)
- 350g flour

### For the filling
- 30g butter
- 50g sugar
- 1 table spoon cinnamon
  
(tbh, this is never enough filling so do more - and especially more cinnamon)

## Instructions
- For the dough, gently heat butter, milk, sugar, cardamom, and salt in a saucepan while stirring until the butter has completely melted. Let cool to body temperature.
- Crumble the yeast into small pieces and stir it into the warm liquid until dissolved. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
- Knead flour and the liquid together in a mixing bowl to form a dough. Cover and let rise for about 1 hour, until doubled in size.
- Roll the dough out on a floured surface into a rectangle the size of a baking sheet. Melt the butter for the filling, spread it over the dough, then mix cinnamon and sugar and sprinkle evenly on top.
- Roll the dough tightly from the long side, cut into 16 slices (I never count them), and place them in a greased baking dish or on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover and let rise for about 30 minutes.
- Preheat the oven to 250°C (top and bottom heat). Brush the rolls with milk and bake for about 6 minutes until golden brown. Enjoy warm if possible.
