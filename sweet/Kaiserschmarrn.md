# Kaiserschmarrn recipe
## Description
a traditional bayarian/aurian/swiss dish that resembels super fluffy bite-sizes pancakes

## Tools
pan
mixer
bowl

## Ingredients:
140g flour
250ml milk
5 eggs
45g sugar
some raisins
1pinch salt
some powdered sugar
as much butter as you like/need for frying

## Instructions
1. seperate the eggs
2. whisk the egg whites
3. mix the egg yellows with the flour, milk and sugar
4. carefully incorperate the egg whites with the rest of the dough
5. melt the butter in the pan
6. add the raising when the butter is hot, just before adding the finished dough into the pan
7. fry it in butter and flip it once done from the first side
8. pull it into smaller pieces once it is nearly done
9. serve with powdered sugar
10. Enjoy!!! :D
